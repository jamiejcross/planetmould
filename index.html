Mouldwire

Mouldwire is a bespoke science/media news service designed to collect and summarise the latest mould related research stories from around the world using a prompt engineered inference engine. This version of Mouldwire is specially calibrated for social science, arts and humanities researchers interested in planetary health, infrastructure, and human/non-human sociality in the Anthropocene.

Mouldwire is an open source project. You can follow the development, view the summaries in raw format, or contribute to the code on Github.
